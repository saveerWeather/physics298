  • Density is defined as mass per unit volume and is a scalar quantity.



  • Units
    • SI            -    kg/m3 or g/cm3
                                            1 kg/m3  =  10-3 g/cm 3
    • British    -    slug/ft3

  • Weight density,  D, often used in the British system



    British system units are lbs/ft3

  • Relative density or specific gravity is the density of a substance relative to the density of water.



    Relative density is a pure number (no units) and has the same value in the SI and British systems.
    e.g.      density of water = 103   kg/m3   ;    density of gold = 19 x 10 3 kg/m3 
               therefore, Relative density of gold = 19
