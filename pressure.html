  • Units
    • SI            -    1 Pascal (Pa) = 1 Newton/m2
    • British    -    1 lb/ft2
    • Other      -    1 Psi = 1 lb/in2
      •                       1 Atmosphere (Atm) = 1.013 x 105 Pa  =  14.7 Psi
                            1 Bar  =  105 Pa,    1 millibar   =  10-3 bar  =  102 Pa   -   Weather applications.
                            1 Torr  =  133 Pa  = pressure exerted by a column of mercury 1mm high  -  blood pressure is measured in torr
       
  • Gauge pressure is the excess pressure above atmospheric pressure.


    • Pabsolute  =  Pgauge  +  P atmospheric
