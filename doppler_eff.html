  • The change in pitch of the sound emitted by a moving vehicle as it passes you is known as the Doppler Effect, named for the Austrian physicist and mathematician Christian Doppler .  We are most familiar with this effect for sound waves, but it is equally applicable to all types of waves.
  • There are two distinct situations we can consider,
    • Source Moving, Observer at Rest:
