Rotational Kinematics

Example Problem

 

A circular saw blade, radius 15 cm, completes 1200 revolutions in 40 seconds while coasting to a stop after being turned off.  Assuming constant deceleration, determine the

            (a)  angular deceleration,

            (b)  initial angular speed and

            (c)  average angular speed.

            (d)  Evaluate the total linear acceleration of a point on the rim of the saw at the start of the deceleration (t = 0).
