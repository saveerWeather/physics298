·        Alternative statement of the first three laws of Thermodynamics:

                    As far as energy is concerned, you can’t win, the best you can do is break even (1st law)

 

              You can only break even at absolute zero (2nd law)

 

              You can’t get to absolute zero (3rd law)

 

          So – in a nutshell – you can’t win !

 

            (Courtesy Rick Marshall, Physics World November 2007)
