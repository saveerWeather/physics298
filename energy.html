ENERGY

  • Definition


"Energy is the ability to do work"
 

  • Types of energy

 

    • Kinetic     -    Energy by virtue of motion


Heat energy of an object is the kinetic energy of the atoms and/or molecules of which the object is comprised.
 

    • Potential     -    Energy by virtue of position


Elastic, electrical and chemical energy are forms of energy defined by the "position" of an object.
 

    • Rest    -    Energy by virtue of mass

 

  • Units of energy    -    same as work

 

    • SI    -    Joules
    • British    -    ft.lbs

 

  • When work is performed, the work appears as one or more form of energy.
    For example, the work performed lifting a book from the floor onto a table appears as an increase in the gravitational potential energy of the book.
