Kinetic Energy and Space Shuttle Columbia

Space Shuttle Orbital Velocity = 17,000 mph (7,500 m/s)

This is about 25 times the speed of sound - Mach 25

Mass of empty shuttle = 68,000 kg (150,000 lbs)

Mass of loaded shuttle = 98,000 kg (215,000 lbs)

We will assume a mass of 85,000kg

Orbital Kinetic Energy of Columbia = 2.4 x 1012 J

This Kinetic Energy, which must be absorbed during re-entry into the atmosphere is

·         Equivalent to 6,000,000 family cars (velocity 60 mph, weight 1.5 tons)

·         Equivalent to 200,000 trucks (velocity 60 mph, weight 40 tons)

·         Equivalent to 500 Boeing 747 Jumbo Jets (velocity 600 mph, mass 160,000 kg)
